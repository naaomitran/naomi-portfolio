find fit friends

An app for people to find friends to do physical activities with. This interface is like a dating app, requiring both users to match in order to connect and message each other, but it’s for finding friends with similar physical activity interests (working out, spin class, walking, hiking, etc.). You can request to be friends, allowing each other to see your posts (photos, comments, schedule – when, where, what you have planned and if you’re looking for someone to join), like a social media platform.

expedia case study

A travel booking website that compiles both flights & accommodations, eliminating the hassle of using multiple platforms when planning your trip! The focus was on enhancing Expedia's user experience by improving filtering usability. The objective was to refine Expedia's filtering system to offer users a more intuitive and efficient way to navigate through travel options.

maritime domain awareness (MDA) platform usability study

MDA platforms monitor and manage maritime activities. A usability study was conducted to analyze market dynamics and evaluate existing platforms' user support. Strategic recommendations, informed by insights and research, were presented for developing Grace—an AI-powered risk assessment tool. Grace emphasizes usability enhancements for unique features to ensure an improved user experience.
