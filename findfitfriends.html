PROBLEM STATEMENT

College provides many opportunities to meet new people and make friends, but post-college results in less proximity and busier schedules, making maintaining friendships harder (Patterson, 2021). Also, staying active is harder post-college due to time and stress from job hunting or working, especially during the transition from studying into a full-time job (Waehner, 2021). Thus, the human activity this system intends to support is to help early grads / young professionals find friends to participate in physical activities together. However, this system can be used by anyone over the age of 18 (due to the nature of online networking) wanting to do physical activity with others.
